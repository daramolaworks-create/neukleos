Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Neukleos collects, uses, protects, and manages information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, company name, project details, and any message you send through our forms or email channels.

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referral source, and website interaction data to help us improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide requested information.
  • To prepare proposals, manage client relationships, and deliver services.
  • To improve our website, content, campaigns, and user experience.
  • To send relevant business communications where permitted.
  • To protect our website, systems, and legitimate business interests.

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand traffic, measure performance, and improve the website experience. You can adjust cookie settings through your browser, though some features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who support our website, analytics, communications, or client operations, provided they handle the information responsibly and only for agreed purposes.

We may also disclose information where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No online system is completely secure, but we work to keep information protected and responsibly managed.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, support business records, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle information, contact us at we@neukleos.com.
